# Ethics and Safety

CareSignal is intentionally designed as a human-in-the-loop support tool.

## What CareSignal does

CareSignal detects interaction patterns that may deserve human attention:

- unanswered questions;
- unresolved blockers;
- signs of overload;
- promised follow-ups;
- useful contributions that may have been ignored.

## What CareSignal does not do

CareSignal does not:

- diagnose users;
- label people as neurodivergent, anxious, vulnerable, disengaged, or at risk;
- evaluate personal character, motivation, disability, or mental health;
- make automated support decisions;
- replace moderators, mentors, teachers, or community leads.

## Data stance

The hackathon demo uses synthetic Slack messages. The live mode can analyze recent messages in the sandbox channel where the app has been installed. The project should not be tested with confidential, sensitive, or real participant data.

## Product principle

CareSignal is not AI that cares. It is AI that helps humans care better.
